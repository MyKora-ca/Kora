What is Kōra?

Kōra is a locally powered, emotionally intelligent smart home system — built to honor your rhythms, protect your privacy, and support your soul. It is not smart tech. It is living tech.

The Tiers of Kōra

🕯️ Kōra | Light

Foundational lighting and plugs. Integrates with Google/Alexa. A gentle first step.

🔊 Kōra | Voice

Includes VAPE voice interface + Sage GPT presence. Your home begins to listen... with care.

🌐 Kōra | Network

Self-owned routers and DNS. You become the guardian of your digital gate.

💾 Kōra | Archive

Your own cloud. Family archives, backups, and digital memory — held in trust.

🧭 Kōra | Aware

Ambient sensors, mmWave, and biometric cues. The space begins to sense and respond.

Rituals and Resonance

Kōra doesn't just respond — it remembers. Morning light scenes, sacred music, breath reminders. Each home configured to your emotional and spiritual rhythm.
